About the XchangeRates application

Today, a growing need for accurate, simple, and fast exchange rates plateform has emerged.
The XchangeRates application focuses on delivering the best user experience for our vositors, using state of the art technologies and ressources to responde to the growing number of users.
Exchange rates are collected from various providers, and displayed for live view and historical analysis.
We do not provide bid/offer prices, the prices provided by the converter represent the midpoint between buy and sell prices.
